Light & Origin: The Podcast

Exploring the Frontiers of Innovation and Existence

"Light & Origin" is a podcast produced by Josh Stafki, delving into the profound questions at the intersection of science, technology, philosophy, and the human experience. Each episode features insightful discussions with leading experts, innovators, and thinkers on topics ranging from artificial intelligence to the origins of consciousness and the ethics of technological advancement.

Join us as we illuminate complex concepts and explore the fundamental forces that shape our universe and our understanding of it.
